What We Do

Our Services

From large-scale commercial builds to residential upgrades and fast service calls — we deliver code-compliant electrical work with a high standard of quality on every job.

Residential Electrical

New construction, remodels, and multi-unit residential wiring done right. From rough-in to final trim, clean work on every home.

  • New construction rough-in
  • Remodels & additions
  • Multi-unit & garage builds
  • Outlets, switches & fixtures

Commercial Electrical

Full-scale commercial systems for offices, retail, warehouses, and industrial facilities — from switchgear to final connections.

  • Power distribution & feeders
  • Warehouse & industrial wiring
  • Lighting design & installation
  • Gear installation & terminations

Panel Upgrades

Replace outdated or undersized panels with modern, safe systems built for today's power demands — done cleanly and to code.

  • Panel replacement & upgrades
  • Load calculations & sizing
  • Sub-panel installation
  • Code compliance & inspection-ready

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 electric vehicle charging stations for homes and commercial parking areas — properly permitted and installed.

  • Level 2 home charger install
  • Commercial multi-stall EV stations
  • Dedicated circuit & panel work
  • Permit & inspection handling

Safety Inspections

Thorough electrical inspections to find hazards, code violations, and inefficiencies before they become expensive or dangerous problems.

  • Full-system electrical inspection
  • Code violation identification
  • Hazard & fire risk assessment
  • Written inspection report

Emergency Repairs

24/7 emergency electrical response. When something goes wrong at your home or business, G3 Power shows up fast and fixes it right.

  • 24/7 emergency response
  • Outage & tripped breaker repair
  • Burning smell & sparking fixes
  • Same-day service available
